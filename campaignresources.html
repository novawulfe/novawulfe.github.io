Youtube Channels

How to be a great GM

A great channel full of tons and tons of videos about DMing, being a better player and tools and tricks you can use. They also do some live streamed and uploaded campaign playthroughs. The video about preparing for specific adventure module campaigns is worth checking out if you plan to play those.

XP to Level 3

Really good channel that has breakdowns of popular roleplayers such as ones from Critical Role and what they bring to the table in terms of RP. Also some really good advice on DMing and lots of homebrew or tweaked ideas for official content.

Dungeon Dudes

These dudes have some amazing videos about being a better player, how to build your character, different ways to role play. They also have indepth videos on how to build specific classes/subclasses and some useful tips about DMing, including rules that often get forgotten or played wrong.

WASD20

Great all round D&D channel, but has an amazing series on map creation, from general mapping rules, how to create interesting looking maps, detailing them etc. Even shows off some live map creation and reviews map making softwares. Useful for any RPG system.

DM Lair

Really good DM tips and tricks with a unique comedy style. He RP's sketches to show points and has some good advice for players too. Also his website often has free one-shots, npcs, magic items and other content up for free.

Taking20

Interesting mix of DM tips, player advice and deep dive looks at items, monsters and things from the D&D books. All round entertaining and educating channel.
