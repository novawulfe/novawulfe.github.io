Feral Druid - Damage

This spec primarly focus's on increasing damage mid to late game, whilst boosting overall survivability from low to high level.

Key Talents/Abilities
  • Brutal Impact - Longer stuns = less damage to you, more time to escape etc.
  • Furor - Essentially gives you a constant stream of rage or energy when shapeshifting for various survival scenarios.
  • Natures Grasp - An instant guaranteed root on a target chasing you.
  • Cure/Abolish Poison - Do not forget this exists, especially when dealing with insect type mobs, nothing worse than finishing a tough fight and still dying because of a poison tick!
  • Barkskin - At 1 min cooldown, use this ability often, do not wait for a panic scenario to use it, it can stop you getting into that scenario to begin with!
Top Races

Tauren - Bonus stamina racial 5%! They get an aoe stun, compared to Night Elf they take the top spot.

BM Hunter - Damage

A spec focusing on maximum early damage when your pet is likely to be weaker, and then buffing your pet to survive and hold agro later.

Key Talents/Abilities
  • Aimed Shot - This is your big nuke and it can be cast at the start of the fight whilst your pet is generating some threat.
  • Intimidation - Guaranteed stun on demand.
  • Spirit Bond - 12% hp a minute doesn't sound like a lot, but it can be super useful to just keep your pet ticking alive during a fight, thats essentially increasing their hp by 12%.
  • Feign Death - If I have to explain this one to you, you probably shouldn't be playing hardcore...
  • /cleartarget [dead]
    /targetenemy [noharm]
    /petattack
     - Use this macro to send your pet to your current target, will save your life when a mob spawns on top of you when fleeing somewhere etc.
Top Races

Most Races - For hunter racial isn't as important, orcs get increased pet damage, dwarfs get a poison/bleed removal, Night Elfs get a free stealth, Trolls get a minor attack speed boost, overall just pick what you feel for this one. Dwarf is probably the strongest to help remove status effects before a feign death.

Frost Mage - Survival

Mages already posses a lot of damage tools as well as some great survivability, this just takes it to the next level for not dying!

Key Talents/Abilities
  • Elemental Precision/Arcane Subtlety - Nothing worse than having someone resist frost nova or polymorph.
  • Frostbite - 15% chance to stun on all frost attacks, this includes frost/ice armor meaning when running away mobs may get rooted in place making your escape easier, other times its just going to boost your dps by keeping mobs away.
  • Iceblock/Icebarrier/Manashield - It's immunity or abosorption to damage, you should be using these all the time.
  • Frost Warding + Arcane Resilience - These combined give you a very nice armour boost especially for late game when a single melee hit could be the difference between a blink cd reset or death
Top Races

Gnome / Undead - Both get great racials for escaping certain crowd control effects, undeads is stronger as blink can already remove roots, but it never hurts to have a backup, being stuck in place is a death sentance for a mage.
